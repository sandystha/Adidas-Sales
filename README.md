# Adidas Sales Analysis Project

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Adidas sales data using **Power BI** to provide insights into sales performance, product trends, and regional performance. The dashboards are fully interactive with **slicers and KPI cards**.

---

## Project Pages

### 1. Sales Page
**Key KPIs:**
- Total Sales: 900M (MoM +9%)  
- Transactions: 10K (MoM +7%)  
- Units Sold: 2M (MoM +7%)  
- Profit: 332M (MoM +10%)  

**Visuals:**
- Sales by Retailer (Top: West Gear – 0.24B)  
- Sales by Product (Top: Men Street Footwear – 0.21B)  
- Sales by Region (Top: West – 0.27B)  
- Sales by Month and State (Region West leads with 2448 orders)  

**Interactive Elements:**
- Slicers for **Year** and **Month**  

---

### 2. Product Analysis Page
**Key KPIs:**
- Total Units Sold: 593K (MoM +7%)  
- Total Sales: 209M (MoM +10%)  

**Features:**
- Product selection using **image/icon slicers**  
- KPI cards that dynamically update based on selection  

---

### 3. Trend Analysis Page
**Key Insights:**
- Sales vs Profit, Sales vs Units Sold, Profit vs Avg Profit Margin  
- Highest profit and sales recorded in **July**  
- Dynamic slicer allows analysis **per store**  

---

## Tools Used
- **Power BI:** Interactive dashboards, KPI cards, slicers, and visualizations  

---

## Key Insights
- West region is the top-performing region  
- Men Street Footwear is the best-selling product  
- July shows peak sales and profit performance  
- Interactive elements make it easy to explore trends by product, month, or store
